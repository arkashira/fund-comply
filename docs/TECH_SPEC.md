## 1. Executive Summary
**Project:** fund-comply
**Objective:** Build an automated compliance and reporting platform for Small and Medium-sized Investment Funds (SME Funds).
**Core Value:** Reduce operational overhead by automating KYC/AML checks, regulatory reporting (e.g., SEC 13F, ESMA MiFID II), and data reconciliation against bank feeds.
