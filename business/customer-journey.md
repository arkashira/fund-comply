# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Fund managers or compliance officers experience regulatory penalties or operational inefficiencies due to manual compliance processes.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about automated compliance tools, complexity of regulatory requirements.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, overwhelm, urgency.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (blogs, webinars) about the benefits of automated compliance.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits from target audience.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Initial research into compliance automation solutions.
- **Friction Points**: Information overload, difficulty comparing solutions, trust in new technology.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful, skeptical, cautious.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear, concise comparison guides, case studies, and testimonials.
- **Metric**: Number of downloads of comparison guides or case studies.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: Decision to trial the automated compliance tool.
- **Friction Points**: Onboarding complexity, integration with existing systems, initial setup time.
- **User Emotions**: Excited, anxious, impatient.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Seamless onboarding process, quick setup, responsive customer support.
- **Metric**: Number of free trial sign-ups, time to first use.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Successful trial period, realization of time and cost savings.
- **Friction Points**: Pricing concerns, long-term commitment, training for staff.
- **User Emotions**: Confident, relieved, satisfied.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Flexible pricing plans, comprehensive training resources, dedicated account manager.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Positive experience with the tool, desire to optimize further.
- **Friction Points**: Scaling the tool across multiple funds, additional feature needs.
- **User Emotions**: Proud, ambitious, eager.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Customized solutions for specific needs, advanced analytics, regular feature updates.
- **Metric**: Number of additional funds or departments adopting the tool, revenue growth from existing customers.
