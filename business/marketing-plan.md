# Marketing Plan
## Positioning
Primary positioning: fund-comply is an automated compliance and reporting tool designed specifically for small and medium-sized investment funds, streamlining regulatory requirements and reducing operational inefficiencies.
Alternative positionings:
1. fund-comply as a regulatory risk management platform, helping investment funds avoid costly penalties and reputational damage.
2. fund-comply as an operational efficiency tool, enabling investment funds to focus on high-value activities by automating routine compliance tasks.
3. fund-comply as a data-driven insights platform, providing investment funds with actionable intelligence to inform strategic decisions and drive business growth.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
1. **Emily Chen**, Compliance Officer at a medium-sized investment fund, responsible for ensuring regulatory adherence and managing a budget of $100,000 for compliance tools and services.
2. **David Lee**, Operations Manager at a small investment fund, tasked with optimizing operational efficiency and allocating a budget of $50,000 for process automation solutions.
3. **Rachel Patel**, Risk Manager at a small to medium-sized investment fund, focused on mitigating regulatory risks and managing a budget of $200,000 for risk management and compliance solutions.

## Channels
Top 3 channels for customer acquisition, along with estimated Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):
1. **LinkedIn Ads**: Targeted advertising on LinkedIn to reach compliance officers, operations managers, and risk managers at small and medium-sized investment funds. Estimated CAC: $500.
2. **Industry Conferences and Webinars**: Sponsorship and participation in conferences and webinars focused on investment fund compliance and regulatory risk management. Estimated CAC: $1,000.
3. **Content Marketing**: Creation and distribution of informative content (blog posts, whitepapers, case studies) on compliance and regulatory risk management for investment funds, published on the fund-comply website and relevant industry platforms. Estimated CAC: $200.

## Content Cadence
Week-by-week content publication plan for the first 12 weeks:
Week 1-2: Introduction to fund-comply and its benefits for small and medium-sized investment funds.
Week 3-4: Regulatory risk management best practices for investment funds.
Week 5-6: Operational efficiency strategies for investment funds.
Week 7-8: Data-driven insights for investment fund compliance and risk management.
Week 9-10: Customer success stories and case studies.
Week 11-12: Industry trends and outlook for investment fund compliance and regulatory risk management.

## Launch Milestones
Key launch milestones:
- **D-30**: Finalize marketing plan, launch content calendar, and initiate LinkedIn Ads campaign.
- **D-0**: Official launch of fund-comply, with press release, email marketing campaign, and sponsored industry webinar.
- **D+30**: Publish first customer success story, and optimize LinkedIn Ads campaign based on initial performance data.
- **D+90**: Review launch performance, assess customer acquisition costs, and plan for future marketing initiatives.

## Success Metrics
Key success metrics:
- **Daily Active Users (DAU)**: Target 100 DAU by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Target $1 million MRR by D+90.
