# Partner Targets

## Integration Roadmap

### 1. **Bloomberg Terminal**
- **Rationale**: Essential for investment funds to access real-time market data and news.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Limited access to basic data; full access requires subscription.
- **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
- **Value-Add**: Provides real-time data for compliance reporting and regulatory updates.
- **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Potential for revenue-sharing agreements with Bloomberg.

### 2. **Refinitiv (LSEG) Data Platform**
- **Rationale**: Comprehensive financial data and analytics for regulatory compliance.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Limited free access to basic datasets; premium data requires subscription.
- **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
- **Value-Add**: Enhances compliance reporting with accurate and up-to-date financial data.
- **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Potential for affiliate partnerships.

### 3. **AWS Compliance Services**
- **Rationale**: Cloud-based compliance and security services for investment funds.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Free tier includes basic compliance checks and limited usage.
- **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
- **Value-Add**: Ensures cloud-based compliance and security for fund operations.
- **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: AWS affiliate program available.

### 4. **Zapier**
- **Rationale**: Automation platform to connect fund-comply with other SaaS tools.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Free tier allows limited automations and tasks per month.
- **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
- **Value-Add**: Simplifies workflow automation and integration with other tools.
- **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Zapier affiliate program available.

### 5. **DocuSign**
- **Rationale**: Electronic signature platform for compliance documentation.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Free tier allows limited document signing per month.
- **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
- **Value-Add**: Streamlines the signing and management of compliance documents.
- **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: DocuSign affiliate program available.

### 6. **Salesforce Financial Services Cloud**
- **Rationale**: CRM platform tailored for financial services and investment funds.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Free trial available; full access requires subscription.
- **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
- **Value-Add**: Enhances customer relationship management and compliance tracking.
- **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Potential for revenue-sharing agreements.

### 7. **Workday Financial Management**
- **Rationale**: Financial management and reporting tools for investment funds.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Free trial available; full access requires subscription.
- **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
- **Value-Add**: Provides comprehensive financial management and reporting capabilities.
- **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: Potential for affiliate partnerships.

### 8. **MuleSoft Anypoint Platform**
- **Rationale**: API-led connectivity platform for integrating various SaaS tools.
- **Free-Tier Limits**: Free tier allows limited API calls and integrations.
- **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
- **Value-Add**: Facilitates seamless integration with multiple SaaS platforms.
- **Affiliate/Revenue-Share**: MuleSoft affiliate program available.
